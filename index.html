Experience the Pulse of Pride

An interactive memory trail celebrating the rich history and vibrant heritage of Ngee Ann Polytechnic.

Explore the Exhibition

Bringing NP's History to Life

"Pulse of Pride" is more than just an exhibition. It's an immersive journey designed to foster a sense of belonging and pride within the NP community. We address the lack of engagement by creating an unforgettable experience for students and staff alike.

Interactive Engagement

Move beyond text-based displays with hands-on activities and a gamified AR tour that makes history exciting and memorable.

Inclusivity & Representation

Celebrating diverse student achievements and perspectives, ensuring every member of the NP community feels seen and valued.

Increased Awareness

Through strategic marketing and exciting incentives, we're making the NP story unmissable and highly accessible.

Interactive Features

AR Gamified Guided Tour

Let our mascot, POP, guide you through an augmented reality adventure. Scan exhibits, listen to audio guides, and complete fun quizzes!

LEGO-Building Activity

What does NP mean to you? Build your vision with LEGO bricks and become part of a collective, evolving art piece.

Digital Photobooth

Unlock the photobooth with your AR tour pass! Snap a pic with our fun templates and share your #PulseOfPrideNP moment.

Faces of Ngee Ann

A dedicated section celebrating the stories and achievements of NP's notable alumni and figures who have shaped our community.

Meet POP the Otter

Our friendly mascot, POP, embodies intelligence, curiosity, and community. Collect free stickers of POP as a souvenir!

Social Media Buzz

Follow our journey on Instagram! Get updates, see behind-the-scenes content, and share your own experience with our community.

App Prototype Preview

Explore a live prototype of our AR Gamified Guided Tour feature, designed to run within the NP Library Mobile App.

Gallery: A Living Mosaic

This space will feature a live collage of photos from our digital photobooth, creating a unique mosaic of the NP community. Your picture could be next!
